Which Character Are You?

Follow the steps below to create your own quiz!

    Step 1: Choose a theme
    Step 2: Choose 4 characters
    Step 3: Fill out template

Step 1: Choose a theme



  • Choose a theme for characters you would like to create your quiz on. This can be any theme you would like!
  • Some examples are: Marvel characters, Disney characters, your favorite superheroes, etc





Continue to Step 2

Step 2: Choose 4 Characters



  • After you have decided on a theme and picked your list of 4 different characters based on your theme.
  • Click on the “Create your own character quiz now!” button when done
